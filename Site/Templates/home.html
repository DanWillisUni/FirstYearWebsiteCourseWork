The Cottage:

A beautiful 2 bedroom cottage with incredible views of the Norfolk Broads. With many local shops and attractions, what's more to love? Free parking comes as standard alongside a private mooring just for your use.

The Cottage includes:

  • 2 bedrooms
  • 3 beds
  • 1 bath
  • Freeview TV
  • bed linen and towels
  • new cutlery and cooking appliances

The Area:

The cottage is located in the capital of the broads: Wroxham. This is the perfect place for exploring the gorgeous waterways comprising the broads. It is also just over 5 miles away from the fine city of Norwich, making it the perfect land and sea exploration post.
